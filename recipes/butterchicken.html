Butter Chicken Recipe

Description

Butter Chicken, or Murgh Makhani, is one of India's most popular dishes — rich, creamy, and full of flavor. It features tender, marinated chicken pieces simmered in a velvety tomato-based sauce with butter, cream, and warm spices.This restaurant-style recipe is perfect for special dinners or whenever you're craving bold Indian flavors. Serve it with steamed basmati rice or warm naan for a complete and satisfying meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb (450 g) boneless chicken thighs, cut into chunks
  • 1/2 cup plain yogurt
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 1 cup tomato puree
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1 tablespoon honey (optional)
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Steps

  1. In a bowl, marinate chicken with yogurt, lemon juice, ginger-garlic paste, garam masala, turmeric, chili powder, and salt. Let sit for at least 30 minutes (or overnight for best flavor).
  2. Heat oil and 1 tablespoon butter in a pan over medium heat. Add marinated chicken and cook until browned and cooked through. Set aside.
  3. In the same pan, melt remaining butter and sauté chopped onions until soft.
  4. Add cumin and coriander, cook for 1 minute. Stir in tomato puree and simmer for 10-15 minutes.
  5. Stir in cream and honey (if using), and return chicken to the pan. Simmer for another 5 minutes.
  6. Garnish with chopped cilantro and serve hot with naan or rice.
